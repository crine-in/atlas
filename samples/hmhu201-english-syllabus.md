Course Objective
This course aims to improve vocabulary, writing skills, and overall communication ability, with
emphasis on clarity, coherence, and correctness in written English.
Detailed Syllabus


Unit 1: Vocabulary Building
• Concept of word formation:
• Compounding
• Backformation
• Clipping
• Blending
• Root words from foreign languages and their use in English
• Prefixes and suffixes from foreign languages to form derivatives• Synonyms and antonyms
• Standard abbreviations and acronyms


Unit 2: Basic Writing Skills
• Sentence structures and types:
• Simple
• Compound
• Complex
• Use of phrases and clauses
• Sentence transformation:
• Active and passive voice
• Direct and indirect narration
• Importance of proper punctuation
• Creating coherence:
• Logical arrangement of sentences and paragraphs
• Creating cohesion:
• Organizing principles in writing
• Techniques for precise writing


Unit 3: Identifying Common Errors in Writing
Subject-verb agreement
Noun-pronoun agreement
Misplaced modifiers
Articles
Prepositions
Redundancies
Clichés


Unit 4: Nature and Style of Effective Writing
Describing
Defining
Classifying
Providing examples and evidence
Writing introductions and conclusions


Unit 5: Writing Practices
Comprehension
Précis writing
Essay writing
Business letter writing
Cover letter and CV
E-mail writing
